Why Preparing is Important
Adventure Prep was made to help normalize Adventure Preperation.
Adventure Preperation is not only needed to ensure safety,
But it also makes the experience more fun and memorable!
Preparing for a trip shifts your mind state to better adapt to what you will face.
You have less of chance of being surprised or nervous on the big day.
How Adventure Prep helps you Prepare for a Trip
The goal of adventure prep is to offer as many free tools that can help people prepare themselves for the outdoors.
We have info on our website that explains how to safely participate in various outdoor activities, click here.
We also have an equipment checklist that can help you organize your equipment necessary for the trip, click here.
If you would like to find some good online information on how to safely go on outdoor trips click here.
We plan on adding socializing features that will alow users to share their trip plans with others.
There will also be a map feature to add geolocation option for trips and a route of your trip.
All these things put together, and I hope that I can one day make a multifaceted social media platform.
The goal is to encourage friends they made online or in person to go on adventures outside in nature.
Whilst also helping people become more aware of the dangers of nature and facilitate their preperation.
Why I Wanted to Make Adventure Prep
The reason I chose to make Adventure Prep is because I experienced the dangers of not being prepared.
One skiing accident in particular deeply shook me, it forced me to rethink how I approach not only skiing but life in general.
The accident I had could have been so easily prevented, simply if I had known that it was a possibility.
Often times the biggest importance for practicing safety is not how well equipped you are,
But rather how informed you are on what the dangers are and what your first course of action given this scenario.
That's why I've always wanted to use an all ecomposing outdoors social media platofrm,
Where you can not only read guides but also see others past trips and be able to learn from real data of real people.
