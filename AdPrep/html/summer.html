Mountain Hiking

Mountain hiking is a thrilling blend of challenge and serenity, catering to adventurers of all skill levels. From gentle slopes for beginners to steep ascents that test the seasoned hiker's mettle, each step unveils breathtaking vistas and a sense of accomplishment. You can be certain to feel revitalized by the fresh air that the thick forests produce, which will have you going back on another hike in no time.

Equipement

Mountain Hiking can vary a lot depending on; what part of the world you are, what time of the year it is and the shape of the mountain. There is winter hiking and summer hiking, and you can climb big or small mountains in either time of the year. For a summer hike we would recommend bringing at least these items:

Hiking List

  • Hiking Boots
  • 2L Water
  • Food and non perishable Snacks
  • Walking Stick
